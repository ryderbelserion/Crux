### Downloading ChatterBox
-> https://modtale.net/mod/chatterbox

### Installation

#### Requirements
:::tip
ChatterBox requires you to use at least **Java 25**
:::

<Steps>
    1. Grab the newest version of ChatterBox
    2. Add it to the `mods` folder
    3. Restart, or start your Hytale Server!
</Steps>
